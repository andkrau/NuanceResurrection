Game Compatibility:

Not all commercial games are supported.  The level of compatibility for the major
commercial releases are described below:

Compatibility Level:
====================

Space Invaders XL
-----------------
Playable, BUT rarely hangs in Battle Mode and Time Attack.

The Next Tetris
-----------------
Playable, distorted music.

Merlin Racing
-------------
Playable, BUT hangs most of the time on/shortly after race start. Also some screen corruption when
watching the Adventure intro.

Tempest 3000
------------
Playable, BUT
1) hangs most of the time when starting a game from certain levels (level 1 as prominent example).
   WORKAROUND enter options->music test first and play some different musics (or disable AudioInterrupts in nuance.cfg :/ )
2) hangs (only if dynamic compilation enabled) on entering level select
   WORKAROUND after(!) doing 1), enter options->vector test first
also music does not loop correctly.

Ballistic
---------
Close to perfect.

Freefall 3050 AD
----------------
Unplayable.  Will require MPEG playback.

Iron Soldier 3
--------------
Unplayable.  Will require MPEG playback.
