Game Compatibility:

Not all commercial games are supported.  The level of compatibility for the major commercial releases are described below:

Compatibility Level:
====================

Space Invaders XL
-----------------
Playable.

The Next Tetris
---------------
Playable, very distorted music/speech.

Merlin Racing
-------------
Playable, BUT some screen corruption when watching the Adventure intro, 
and minor gfx glitches during the race.

Tempest 3000
------------
Playable, BUT hangs (only if dynamic compilation enabled) on entering level select.
WORKAROUND: Just enter 'options'->'vector test' first, before doing anything else.

Ballistic
---------
Close to perfect.

Crayon Shin-Chan 3
------------------
Playable.

Bust A Move 4
-------------
Unreleased so far, but exists. Thus not tested.

Freefall 3050 A.D.
------------------
Unplayable.  Will require MPEG playback, and proper reloading of code, etc.

Iron Soldier 3
--------------
Unplayable.  Will require MPEG playback, and proper reloading of code, etc.
